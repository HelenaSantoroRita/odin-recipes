Classic Pasta Cacio e Pepe

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces spaghetti or tonnarelli
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, cubed, divided
  • 1 teaspoon freshly cracked black pepper
  • 3/4 cup finely grated Pecorino Romano
  • 3/4 cup finely grated Parmesan
  • Salt, to taste

Directions

  1. Cook the Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook, stirring occasionally, until about 2 minutes before tender. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water, then drain the pasta.
  2. Melt Butter: In a large pan, melt 2 tablespoons of butter. Add black pepper and cook until toasted, about 1 minute.
  3. Combine Pasta and Butter: Add half of the reserved pasta water to the pan. Bring to a simmer. Add pasta and remaining butter. Reduce heat to low and add Pecorino, stirring and tossing with tongs until melted. Remove pan from heat; add Parmesan and toss until cheese melts, sauce coats the pasta, and pasta is al dente. Add more pasta water if sauce seems dry.
  4. Season and Serve: Transfer pasta to warm bowls and serve.
