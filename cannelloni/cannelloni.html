Classic Cannelloni Recipe

A tipycal Italian pasta to eat with family onb sunday

Ingredients

For the Filling:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 cup spinach, chopped (optional)
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg

For the Tomato Sauce:

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano

Additional Ingredients:

  • 12 cannelloni tubes
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated

Directions

  1. Prepare the Filling: In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, sautéing until soft. Add ground beef, cooking until browned. Mix in spinach until wilted, then transfer to a mixing bowl. Let cool slightly before mixing in ricotta cheese. Season with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
  2. Make the Tomato Sauce: In the same skillet, heat additional olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic, cooking until fragrant. Stir in crushed tomatoes, salt, pepper, sugar, and oregano. Simmer for about 10 minutes until slightly thickened.
  3. Fill the Cannelloni: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Spoon the filling into a piping bag or a plastic bag with a corner cut off, and fill each cannelloni tube. Arrange the filled tubes in a single layer in a baking dish.
  4. Assemble: Pour the tomato sauce over the filled cannelloni. Sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses.
  5. Bake: Cover the dish with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  6. Serve: Let the cannelloni cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy!
